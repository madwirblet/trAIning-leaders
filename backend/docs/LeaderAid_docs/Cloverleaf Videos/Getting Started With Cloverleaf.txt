High-performing, inclusive teams deliver winning business results. By helping employees to understand themselves and their co-workers, we can build thriving teams. Cloverleaf is a platform for you and your co-workers to take personality and communications assessments like 16 types, DISC, Enneagram, Instinctive Drives, and Strengthscope.

Get your personal insights for better awareness of your strengths, motivations, and communication style. Easily see the strengths of everyone on the team and the similarities and differences between each team member. Cloverleaf delivers coaching tips to help you understand yourself and work better with your team. 

Managers use insights from Cloverleaf to motivate and coach employees to produce stronger work. Ready to get started? Choose an assessment. Easily click or tap through the assessment form. 

Once it's complete, you'll have access to insights about yourself. Scan through the me dashboard to read coaching tips about your strengths, blind spots, communication, and leadership styles. You'll also get personalized coaching tips about yourself and your team and your email throughout the week. 

Connect your calendar to receive daily insights on co-workers with Cloverleaf profiles before you meet with them. Use the directory to look up team members and learn more about them. Want to dig even deeper? Check out the downloadable reports to see a full analysis of your results for greater self-awareness. 

Head over to the team dashboard to learn more about everyone on the team. At a glance, see each team member's strengths and their unique value and contribution. Want to compare two members of the team? Use relationship maps to compare any two members, including yourself. 

Use Cloverleaf to help your team thrive and produce stronger work that we're all proud of.
